# Research Data Notes

This folder keeps raw research notes and data points that may be used later in the paper or presentation.

## Files

- `key-quotes.md`: short evidence notes from IEA, urban ventilation corridor cases, and serious game examples.
- `key-statistics.md`: compact list of statistics and data fields that support the MVP design.

## Current MVP Export Fields

- `budgetUsed`: how much budget the player used.
- `score`: simplified score based on temperature, wind, and carbon emissions.
- `metrics.temp`: average temperature.
- `metrics.wind`: average wind speed.
- `metrics.carbon`: carbon emissions.
- `layout`: each facility's x/y position, terrain, and facility type.
- `survey.budgetChoice`: whether the player chose coal to save budget.
- `survey.windUnderstanding`: whether the player understood that coal at wind outlets reduces ventilation.
